Senior PHP Developer

Tech Solutions Inc

New York, USA Full-time $80,000 - $120,000/year Posted 2 days ago
Full-time Senior Remote Available

Job Description

We are looking for an experienced PHP Developer to join our team. You will be responsible for developing and maintaining web applications using PHP and related technologies.

As a Senior PHP Developer, you will work closely with our design and product teams to create elegant, efficient, and scalable solutions. You'll be involved in all stages of the development lifecycle, from planning and design to implementation and deployment.

Responsibilities

  • Develop, test, and maintain web applications using PHP frameworks (Laravel, Symfony)
  • Write clean, well-documented, and efficient code
  • Design and implement database schemas with MySQL/PostgreSQL
  • Collaborate with front-end developers to integrate user-facing elements
  • Participate in code reviews and mentor junior developers
  • Troubleshoot, debug, and upgrade existing applications
  • Stay up-to-date with emerging technologies and industry trends
  • Contribute to technical documentation

Requirements

  • Minimum 5 years of professional PHP development experience
  • Strong proficiency in PHP 7.x/8.x and modern frameworks (Laravel preferred)
  • Solid understanding of MySQL/PostgreSQL database design and optimization
  • Experience with RESTful API design and implementation
  • Familiarity with front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Knowledge of version control systems (Git)
  • Experience with testing frameworks (PHPUnit)
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field (or equivalent experience)

Nice to Have

  • Experience with Vue.js or React
  • Knowledge of Docker and containerization
  • Experience with AWS or other cloud platforms
  • Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines
  • Experience with microservices architecture

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and performance bonuses
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • 401(k) with company matching
  • Flexible work hours and remote work options
  • Professional development budget
  • Paid time off and holidays
  • Modern office with free snacks and drinks
  • Team building events and activities
